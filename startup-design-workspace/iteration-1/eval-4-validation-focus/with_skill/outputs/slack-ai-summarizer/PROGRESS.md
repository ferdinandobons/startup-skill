## Notes

- The founder explicitly asked for a go/no-go validation assessment. Intake was conducted without interactive interview since the request was provided as a single message.
- Intake acknowledges the existing 23/30 interview data but flags significant methodological gaps that affect how much weight the data can carry.
- Brainstorm explored 7 variations and converged on a recommendation to shift form factor (Chrome extension to Slack bot), shift positioning (summarization to prioritization), and validate more rigorously before committing budget to development.
- Key follow-up questions were documented in the brief for the founder to address before proceeding to research.
- No web search was used; research phase would benefit from competitive intelligence on Slack AI features, existing Slack bots in the summarization/productivity space, and pricing benchmarks.
