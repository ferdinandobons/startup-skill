# Lean Canvas — Smart Parking Finder

## Problem (Top 3)
1. **Wasted time circling for parking.** Urban drivers in cities like San Francisco and Los Angeles spend an average of 15-20 minutes per trip searching for parking, leading to frustration, wasted fuel, and increased congestion.
2. **No real-time visibility into parking availability.** Existing solutions rely on static data or outdated information. Drivers have no reliable way to know which spots are open before arriving.
3. **Parking garage operators lack demand data.** Operators cannot optimize pricing or capacity because they have limited insight into real-time demand patterns across their facilities.

## Customer Segments
- **Primary:** Daily commuters in cities with 500k+ population (starting with San Francisco and Los Angeles) who drive to work or appointments and face recurring parking frustration.
- **Secondary:** Visitors and tourists unfamiliar with local parking options, willing to pay for convenience.
- **B2B:** Parking garage and lot operators who want to increase occupancy and optimize revenue.

## Unique Value Proposition
Find and reserve a parking spot before you arrive — using live sensor data, not guesswork.

## Solution
- Real-time parking availability map powered by IoT sensors and camera feeds
- Spot reservation system integrated with participating garages and lots
- Turn-by-turn navigation to the chosen spot
- Premium tier offering access to reserved spots and priority availability alerts

## Channels
- App stores (iOS and Android) with ASO targeting "parking" keywords
- Partnerships with parking garage operators who promote the app to their customers
- City transportation department partnerships and commuter program integrations
- Google Maps / Apple Maps integration or advertising alongside navigation queries
- Local SEO and SEM targeting "[city] parking" search queries

## Revenue Streams
- **Consumer subscription:** $9.99/month premium tier for reservations and premium spot access
- **B2B revenue share:** Commission on reservations made through the platform (estimated 10-15% of transaction value)
- **Data licensing:** Aggregated parking demand and traffic pattern data sold to city planners and urban developers (long-term play)
- **Freemium conversion:** Free tier drives adoption; conversion to paid via reservation convenience

## Cost Structure
- IoT sensor procurement and installation partnerships
- Cloud infrastructure for real-time data processing (high compute needs for camera/sensor feeds)
- App development and maintenance (iOS, Android, backend)
- Customer acquisition (digital marketing, partnership development)
- City-by-city regulatory compliance and legal
- Operational staff for partner management and support

## Key Metrics
- **Active users:** Daily and monthly active drivers using the app
- **Spots indexed:** Number of parking spots with live availability data
- **Reservation rate:** Percentage of sessions that result in a reservation
- **Freemium-to-paid conversion rate:** Target 5-8% within first year
- **Time-to-park reduction:** Average minutes saved per session vs. baseline
- **Partner garage occupancy lift:** Measurable increase in occupancy for partnered locations
