# Smart Parking Finder — Intake Brief

## The Idea
An app that uses real-time data from IoT sensors and camera feeds to help drivers find available parking spots in busy urban areas. Shows live availability, lets you reserve spots, and provides navigation.

## Target Market
- Urban drivers in cities with 500k+ population
- Starting with San Francisco and Los Angeles
- Primary: daily commuters, Secondary: visitors/tourists

## Founder
- Solo founder, software engineer with 8 years experience
- Previously worked at Waymo on sensor data
- Full-time, $50k savings

## Business Model
- Freemium: free basic search, $9.99/mo for reservations and premium spots
- B2B: partnerships with parking garage operators (revenue share)
- Long-term: data licensing to city planners

## Constraints
- Needs IoT sensor partnerships or camera access
- Privacy concerns with camera data
- City-by-city regulatory approval needed
